# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do's

- **Think before you post** — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with a future employer, professor, or family member seeing it.
- **Use privacy settings** — regularly review who can see your posts, stories, and personal information.
- **Give credit** — always attribute quotes, images, or ideas that are not your own.
- **Be respectful in disagreements** — engage with differing opinions calmly and constructively.
- **Verify before sharing** — fact-check news, statistics, or claims before forwarding them to others.

## ❌ Don'ts

- **Don't overshare personal information** — avoid posting your location, address, phone number, or daily schedule publicly.
- **Don't post when emotional** — avoid sending or publishing something in anger or distress; wait and reflect first.
- **Don't screenshot and share private conversations** — this breaks trust and can have serious social or legal consequences.
- **Don't engage with or spread cyberbullying** — do not like, share, or participate in content that humiliates or targets someone.
- **Don't ignore your digital footprint** — remember that deleted posts can still be screenshotted or cached; your online history follows you.
