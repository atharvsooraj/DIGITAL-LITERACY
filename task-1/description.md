# DESCRIPTION OF INFPGRAPHIC
For this project, I used Canva to design my infographic, taking advantage of its drag-and-drop layout tools, built-in icon library, and customisable templates. My infographic presents information visually through
a combination of icons, bold headings, colour-coded sections, and brief text blocks arranged in a clear hierarchy. Each section is designed to guide the reader's eye from the main topic at the top down through
supporting details and key facts, making the content easy to scan and understand at a glance.
One thing I found interesting while making it was deciding how to balance text and visuals too much text made it look cluttered, while too little made it hard to understand. I had to experiment with font sizes
and spacing to ensure the information was readable without overwhelming the design. I also found it challenging to keep the colour scheme consistent while still making each section visually distinct from the
others.
